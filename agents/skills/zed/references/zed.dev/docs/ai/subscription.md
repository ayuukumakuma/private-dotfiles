# [Subscription](https://zed.dev/docs/ai/subscription.html#subscription)

Zed's hosted models are offered via subscription to Zed Pro or Zed Business.

> You can use [your own API keys](https://zed.dev/docs/ai/llm-providers.html) or [external agents](https://zed.dev/docs/ai/external-agents.html) without a Zed subscription.

See the following pages for specific aspects of our subscription offering:

* [Models](https://zed.dev/docs/ai/models.html): Overview of the models offered by Zed's subscriptions.
* [Plans and Usage](https://zed.dev/docs/ai/plans-and-usage.html): Outlines Zed's plans and how usage is measured.
* [Billing](https://zed.dev/docs/ai/billing.html): Billing policies and procedures, and how to update or view various billing settings.
